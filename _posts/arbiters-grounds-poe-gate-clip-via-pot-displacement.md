## Summary

This clip uses [Displacement Clipping](/posts/displacement-clipping) to enter collision near the Poe gate, then uses a rupee clawshot pickup to push fully through the gate barrier.

## Setup

1. Reach the Poe gate room with pot displacement access.
2. Set pot position and Link angle for the displacement push.
3. Use the small bubble/texture cues to line up side-specific positioning.

![Poe gate clip cue (west)](/assets/glitches/arbiters-grounds-poe-gate-clip-via-pot-displacement/bubble-clip-cue-west.png)

## Execution

1. Perform the pot displacement to get partial wall entry.
2. Adjust into the best angle for the final push.
3. Clawshot the rupee pickup to complete the clip through the Poe gate.
