## Summary

Opening the item wheel on the exact frame Puppet Zelda dialogue appears can hard-crash the game.

## Trigger

1. Reach the Puppet Zelda sequence in Hyrule Castle.
2. Press to open the item wheel on the same frame the Puppet Zelda text appears.
3. If timed correctly, the game crashes immediately.
