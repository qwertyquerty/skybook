## Summary

By performing a frame perfect jump or side hop during Uli's dialogue, Link can defer the dialogue continuing. Then, mid-air, the player can save/quit out of the dialogue. Upon returning, the Fishing Rod obtained dialogue reappears, though only one appears in inventory.
