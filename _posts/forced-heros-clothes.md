## Summary

Multiple transitions can force Hero's Clothes:
- Doing BiT puts you in Hero's Clothes.
- Teleporting back to Ordon from Hyrule Castle puts you in Hero's Clothes.
- Clearing Faron Twilight forces Hero's Clothes.
- Getting the Master Sword can force Hero's Clothes as Wolf, or crash in Ordon Clothes.
